Calorie Counter

The place where you keep track of your health

You can log your meals and Calorie Counter will keep track of your eating habits. When you register to the web app it calculates the calories your body needs for the day. Each day you should log the food you have eaten. You can choose from our Food Plans what to eat for the day to reach the calorie goal. You are also able to track your burnt calories by logging your activities, they will be added to your total calories if you want so. Calorie Counter makes statistics based on your calorie intake and the level of your activity. You are able to check them and better your health.

Counting Calories

Why should I count my calories?

Calorie counting can be educational. Calorie counting forces us to more closely monitor what we put in our mouths. It can be a very eye-opening exercise and help people become more mindful when it comes to eating. It can help people understand exactly how to portion their food in a new way by actually learning how much energy is in the food they consume. It gets people to turn over their food packages.

Calories are the easiest number to see on the label and can lead to learning about the other nutrients as well. It helps you eliminate excess calories, which are what always lead to weight gain. The only way to lose weight is to eat fewer calories than you burn, and calorie counting helps you track those numbers.
